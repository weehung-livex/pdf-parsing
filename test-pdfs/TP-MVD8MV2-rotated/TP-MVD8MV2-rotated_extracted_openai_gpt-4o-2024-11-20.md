- **Angle of View (V)**: 54.2° ~ 16.3°  
- **Angle of View (O)**: 111.0° ~ 33.1°  
- **Adjustment angle**:

  - Tilt: 0° ~ 75°  
  - Rotate: 0° ~ 360°  
- **Shutter**: Auto/Manual, 1 ~ 1/100000s  
- **Minimum Illumination**:

- **IR Range**: Up to 40m (131ft) IR range

- **IR On/Off Control**: Auto/Manual  
- **Defog**: Digital Defog  
- **WDR**: 120dB
